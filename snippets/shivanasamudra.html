Shivanasamudra
Shivanasamudra is a small island town in the Mandya district of Karnataka. Situated on the banks of the River Cauvery, the area is surrounded by forested hills and lush green valleys. Here the Cauvery River winds its way through the Deccan Plateau and drops downstream to form the beautiful twin waterfalls. The western stream is known as Gaganachukki while the eastern one is known as Bharachukki. This is the waterfall where Asiaâ€™s first hydroelectric power station was set up in the year 1905. Watching the river falling down into a cloud of foaming spray is a breathtaking experience. These falls are the second biggest waterfall in India and the sixteenth largest in the world.
