Bangalore
Bengalore is the capital of Karnataka and a hub for high tech industries. It has everything that you ask for, in order to let you have a wonderful time. You can go shopping, visit a wildlife sanctuary, spend a quiet evening in a park or hangout at one of the glitzy malls. It is a cosmopolitan city and chances are that you will fall in love with the city sooner or later. There are heritage sites and religious ones, museums, lakes, art galleries and plenty of other opportunities for having a great time.

Few places you would love to visit in Bangalore are,
  • Cubbon Park
  • Lalbagh Gardens
  • Jamia Masjid
  • National Gallery of Modern Art
  • Jawaharlal Nehru Planetarium
  • Karnataka Chitrakala Parishad
  • Ulsoor Lake
